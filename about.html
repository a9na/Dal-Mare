About Us

Apartments Dal Mare is your serene escape in Grebaštica, a beautiful town in Dalmatia. Offering modern apartments with stunning views and close proximity to the Adriatic, we are dedicated to providing you with a luxurious and memorable stay.

Whether you're here to relax by the sea, explore historic towns, or enjoy local cuisine, Apartments Dal Mare is the perfect base for your Dalmatian adventure.
